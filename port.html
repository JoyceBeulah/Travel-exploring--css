Explore South Indian Hills

Your Guide to the Majestic Hills of South India

Welcome to South India's Scenic Hill Stations

Embark on an adventure through the serene hills, with trekking and jeep safari experiences.

Featured Hill Stations

Ooty - Queen of Hill Stations

Experience the cool weather, lush tea plantations, and serene lakes of Ooty. This charming hill station is perfect for trekking, nature walks, and picturesque jeep safaris.

Munnar - The Tea Haven

Discover the rolling hills, verdant tea gardens, and breathtaking landscapes of Munnar. Ideal for both trekking and jeep safari, Munnar offers a peaceful retreat into nature.

Coorg - The Scotland of India

Immerse yourself in the misty hills, coffee plantations, and cascading waterfalls of Coorg. Enjoy thrilling treks, jeep safaris, and the tranquil beauty of this hill station.

Yercaud - The Jewel of the South

Known for its beautiful orange groves and coffee plantations, Yercaud is a serene escape. Ideal for a relaxed getaway with opportunities for trekking and exploring its natural beauty.

Coonoor - A Quaint Retreat

Located near Ooty, Coonoor offers a quieter, more laid-back experience. Explore its tea estates, enjoy the scenic jeep rides, and trek through the lush Nilgiri hills.

Kotagiri - The Hidden Gem

Kotagiri is one of the oldest hill stations in the Nilgiris. Known for its pleasant weather and picturesque landscapes, it’s perfect for nature enthusiasts looking for trekking and peaceful retreats.

Kolli Hills - The Untouched Beauty

With its stunning waterfalls, dense forests, and serene surroundings, Kolli Hills is a lesser-known paradise. It's a great spot for adventurous treks and off-the-beaten-path jeep safaris.

Kolukkumalai - The Highest Tea Estate

Kolukkumalai is famous for having the highest tea plantations in the world. The scenic beauty, combined with challenging treks and breathtaking jeep safaris, makes it a must-visit.

Wayanad - A Verdant Paradise

Wayanad, with its lush forests, wildlife sanctuaries, and misty hills, offers a tranquil escape. Ideal for trekking enthusiasts and those seeking a close encounter with nature.

Vagamon - A Serene Hideaway

Vagamon, nestled in the Western Ghats, is known for its rolling meadows, pine forests, and adventure activities like paragliding. It’s perfect for serene treks and peaceful jeep rides.

Chikmagalur - The Coffee Land

Chikmagalur, famous for its coffee plantations and rugged terrains, offers a perfect blend of adventure and relaxation. Trek through its hills or take a jeep safari through the coffee estates.
